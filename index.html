HOW TO PLAY

JUST A BRIEF

→ Math Quiz is a 2 Playered game which can be played with a friend or a parent.
→ Children above 4 can play this game.
→ It enhances your knowledge in Maths.

STARTING WITH THE GAME

⦿ Start by Logging in; you and your partner.
⦿ Turns for giving the question and answering the question will come one-by-one.

HOW TO GIVE QUESTIONS

⦾ E.g. - First turn is your to give the question.
⦾ Enter 2 numbers for your partner each in the box.
⦾ They can be any numbers; 2 and 6, 100 and 345, 5 and 7, anything...
⦾ After entering click the button and the game will give either +, -, ÷, x to your partner from the desired numbers you selected.

HOW TO ANSWER

⋆ The question your partner gave is what you have to answer.
⋆ For God's sake please do not use any online websites, calculators or the help of somebody.
⋆ Type the answer and click on 'Check'.
⋆ If the answer is correct, It will add a point in front of your name.
⋆ If not, No points are added.
